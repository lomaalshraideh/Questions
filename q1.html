About Coding Academy

The Coding Academy by Orange, inaugurated in June 2019, in collaboration with Simplon.co, embarked on its mission to provide comprehensive, tuition-free 6-month training courses in coding languages, complemented by a month-long internship within tech and tech-enabled companies. year, students are selected to join the Academy through a competitive admissions process.

“Train and Empower Youth to enhance their opportunities in the digital sector in Jordan.”
